## FAQ

**Section title:** `Questions, answered`  
**Section subtitle:** `Straight answers for teams who live in AI tools every week.`

---

### Q1

**Q:** `Is the audit really free?`  
**A:** `Yes. No credit card, no forced signup for the core flow. You enter your stack, we show savings opportunities and a report you can share. We may offer optional consultation if your potential savings are large—that’s always your choice.`

---

### Q2

**Q:** `Which tools can I include?`  
**A:** `Today we support Cursor, ChatGPT, Claude, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, OpenAI API, Anthropic API, and Windsurf. We expand the catalog as pricing and plans stabilize—if your vendor’s missing, tell us and we’ll prioritize it.`

---

### Q3

**Q:** `How accurate are the savings numbers?`  
**A:** `Estimates come from published list pricing and the plans you tell us you’re on. Enterprise discounts, annual commits, and usage-heavy API bills can all differ—use the output as a directional baseline, then validate against your contracts.`

---

### Q4

**Q:** `What happens to my data?`  
**A:** `Audits live in a managed database with access controls. Your report is reachable via a unique link—anyone with the link can view that audit, so treat it like a private doc until we ship tighter sharing controls. We don’t sell your data.`

---

### Q5

**Q:** `Can I share this with finance or leadership?`  
**A:** `That’s the point. Copy the report link, drop it in Slack or email, and let the chart do the talking. No PII required to run the audit itself.`

---

### Q6

**Q:** `How often should we run this?`  
**A:** `Quarterly is a good rhythm—or any time you add a tool, renew a contract, or onboard a big batch of seats. AI pricing shifts constantly; a stale stack model is an expensive one.`
